What is RSYC?

RSYC is a Car Show Room Application to simulate and manage the sale of cars in a store. How the application works is when a customer arrive at the store are presented in a queue and the queue can only receive a maximum of 10 customers at any one time. Only the first customer in queue can be move, the others must wait for their turn. The application allows the first customer in the queue to be moved to a car according to their preference (Porsche, Volkswagen, Audi or BMW). When the first customer in the queue is moved to a car, a new customer can enter the queue.

How to play the application

Step 1

Click on the START button if you wish to purchase/rent a car from Rauf Sells Your Cars. If you do click on the button, be sure to enjoy the sound of Audi A294 Type 48 engine startup sound.

Step 2

Drag the first customer to the car based on the brand. For example if the customer wants Porsche, drag the customer and drop them onto a car from Porsche. We specifically designed this application to only allow you to drag the customer at the top each time.

Step 3

After dropping the customers accordingly to their brands, You will be asked to answer 3 MCQ questions about the company. You will get a pass if you get all 3 or 2 questions correct. If you fail to do so, the customer will automatically exit the store.

Step 4

Congratulations on passing the MCQ Quiz. Now what is left is for you to decide whether you really want to purchase the car by clicking Yes or if you have second thoughts and want to exit you may do so by clicking No and Exit.
